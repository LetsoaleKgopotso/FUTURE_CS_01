# FUTURE_CS_01
Welcome to my Future Interns repository. 
This is the first Task where I performed a Read Only Vulnerability Assessment on the portal of Gauteng Professional Job Centre (GPG). 

Having detected a total service outage on the portal, I took the responsibility of doing a technical audit that will detect the root of the problem and ensure that the important service remains stable and safe for use by job-seeking citizens in South Africa. 

Some highlights of my vulnerability assessment are:
1.  Passive Reconnaissance: Utilization of Kali Linux and Nmap tools for detection of exposed services and vulnerabilities within infrastructure.
2.  Configuration Audit: Discovery of significant "Information Disclosure" vulnerabilities and out-of-date framework modules.
3.  Web Hardening: Auditing of HTTP Security Headers (HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options) to ensure Defense-in-Depth measures.
4.  Actionable Remediation: Converting technical weaknesses to business solutions. 

This approach makes processes more efficient as it enables IT personnel to transition from being reactive towards technical issues to being proactive in terms of securing infrastructure and ensuring uptime of critical services.
